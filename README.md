# pictchme
Here's your complete interactive Teen Summit Shark Tank Game page! It's a fully self-contained HTML file students can open in any browser. Here's what's packed in:

**Game Structure:**
- **Role Picker** — Students choose Entrepreneur or Shark Investor with clear task lists for each
- **"What IS a Business?"** — Tabbed educational section covering basics, 10 startup steps, how money works, and common mistakes to avoid

**The 5 Key Questions** — Each with explanations and strong example answers:
1. What is your business?
2. Who will buy it?
3. Cost to start?
4. Selling price?
5. Why will it succeed?
Plus a bonus section on how investors profit from deals.

**12 Business Ideas** — All between $1–$10 startup cost with step-by-step instructions, profit margins, and selling prices

**6 Full Sample Pitches** — Complete Shark Tank-ready pitch cards (Bracelet Boss, Snack Attack, Study Star Kits, Slime Factory, Sticker Studio, Cookie Crew) each answering all 5 questions with real numbers and investor return calculations

**Interactive Tools:**
- Shark Evaluation Scorecard with Yes/Maybe/No checklist
- Deal decision buttons (I'm In / I'm Out / Let's Negotiate)
- Build Your Own Pitch form with a live profit calculator
- Money Tracker with expense and sales logging
- 16-term business vocabulary section
- Classroom timeline for teachers to run the game
